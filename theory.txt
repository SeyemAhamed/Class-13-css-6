

========Position=======

Position: (static/relative/absoulate/fixed)

static: default (top, right, bottom, left will not be worked)

relative: element related to itself (top, right, bottom, left)

absoulate: Position relative to it's parent(ancestor) (top,right, bottom, left )

fixed: position relative to it's browser

sticky: position depends on scroll

top, right, bottom, left
